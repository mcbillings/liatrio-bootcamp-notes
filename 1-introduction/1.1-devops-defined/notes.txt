Section 1.1 Notes
=================

DevOps generally serves as a method for easing tension and bottlenecks associated with the overall development process-- All the way from development and into production.

There are several acronyms and concepts that are all affiliated with this goal

The Three Ways
--------------
1. Systems Thinking
2. Amplifying Feedback Loops
3. Culture of Continuous Experimentation and Learning

CAMS
--------------
(C)ulture
(A)utomation
(M)easurement
(S)haring

ICE
-------------
(I)nclusivity
(C)omplex Systems
(E)mpathy
